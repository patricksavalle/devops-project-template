## Best Practices

- [ ] Add tracing (request lifecycle)


- [ ] Add logging


- [ ] Monitor the golden four signals 
  - Latency
  - Error rate
  - Traffic
  - Saturation


- [ ] As simple as possible, no simpler


- [ ] Avoid false positives at all cost, all alerts must be actionable


- [ ] Keep dashboard stupid simple
